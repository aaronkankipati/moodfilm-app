Privacy Policy — Moodfilm

Last updated: 25 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Moodfilm app ("Moodfilm", "the app", "we") collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. By using Moodfilm you agree to this policy.

1. Who we are

Moodfilm is operated by Aaron Kankipati. For any privacy question, contact us at Kankipati.aaron@gmail.com.

2. We do not require an account

Moodfilm has no sign-up, no login, and does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other directly identifying personal information.

3. Information the app collects

  • A random device identifier. The first time you open the app, it generates a random ID (for example d_lq8x2_a93kf01) and stores it on your device. This ID is not linked to your identity. It is sent to our server so the app can remember how many searches you have used and whether you have an active purchase.
  • Your mood input. The text you type or the mood tags you pick, your selected "vibe", and your language preference are sent to our server so the app can generate film recommendations.
  • Approximate country. The app reads your device's time zone / language setting to guess your country, so it can show which streaming services have a film in your region. It does not collect your precise location.
  • Purchase information. If you buy credits or a subscription, Google Play processes the payment and provides our server with a purchase token so we can unlock what you paid for. We never see or store your card details.

4. How your information is used

Information is used only to operate the app: to generate film recommendations, to count free and paid searches, to deliver purchased credits or subscriptions, and to keep the service working. We do not use it for advertising and we do not sell it.

5. Third-party services

To provide its features, Moodfilm sends limited data to the following services:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI): your mood text is processed by Anthropic's AI to understand it and choose films. See Anthropic's privacy policy.
  • The Movie Database (TMDb): used to fetch film details, posters, and streaming availability. See TMDb's privacy policy.
  • Cloudflare: hosts the server (the "Worker") that handles app requests.
  • Google Play Billing: processes all purchases. See Google's privacy policy.

6. Data retention

Your device identifier and search-count information are kept on our server for as long as needed to run the service. Mood text is processed to generate your recommendations and is not used to build a long-term profile of you. You can remove the device identifier at any time by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app.

7. Children

Moodfilm is not directed at children and is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Your choices

Because we hold no account and no identifying information, the simplest way to remove all data associated with your device is to clear the app's storage or uninstall the app. To request deletion of server-side data tied to your device identifier, contact us at the email above and include your device identifier if known.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will always reflect the latest version.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to Kankipati.aaron@gmail.com.
