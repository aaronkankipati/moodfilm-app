Privacy Policy for Moodfilm

Effective Date: May 21, 2026

Moodfilm ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application, Moodfilm.

1. Information Collection and Use

Moodfilm is designed to be a privacy-first application. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data or personally identifiable information (PII). Any data processing required for the app to function happens locally on your device.

2. Third-Party Services

To provide specific features and support the application, we utilize trusted third-party services. These external services may process data according to their own privacy policies. The third-party services used by Moodfilm include:

  • Google AdMob: Used for displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect and use pseudonymous identifiers (like your device's advertising ID) to serve relevant ads and monitor ad performance.
    View Google's Privacy Policy
  • The Movie Database (TMDb): Used to fetch movie metadata, imagery, and details. No personal data is shared with TMDb.
    View TMDb's Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic: Used to power AI-driven movie recommendations based on mood inputs. Mood queries are processed strictly to generate suggestions and are not linked to your personal identity.
    View Anthropic's Privacy Policy

3. Children's Privacy

Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
